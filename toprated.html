TOP RATED

Here is a list of the top rated restaurants in Amsterdam. This list is strictly based on customers reviews and ratings.

CAFE VRIJDAG

Our restaurant and terrace is open daily! Come along for the ultimate Friday feeling, whatever day of the week it is. Book your table on the spot or make a reservation online (only possible for indoors).

RESTAURANT MERAM

Meram is a Turkish restaurant with branches in every area of Amsterdam, from East to West and North to South. Meram is known for their delicious Turkish food since 1999.

SAMPURNA

Sampurna is a Indonesian cuisine diner where small and large groups of friends and families meet to enjoy the excellent food, wine and the relaxed ambiance for over 35 years.

THE BLACK DOG

The Black Dog is a friendly bar & restaurant on the beautiful Spuistraat with an extensive beer and drinks selection and an interesting menu of streetfood from around the world and pub classics.
